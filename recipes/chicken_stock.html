Chicken Stock

Delicious Chicken Stock for winter nights

This warm and salty chicken stock will make you feel warm on those cold winter nights

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Salt
  • Water
    Steps
  • Clean chicken thighs
  • Add water to pot
  • Add chicken tighs
  • Boil for 30 min
  • Add salt and spices
  • Let simmer for 25 min
  • Enjoy
