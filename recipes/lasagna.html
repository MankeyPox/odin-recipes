Delicious Lasagna

Lasagna quick and easy with this handy recipe

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato
  • Ground Beef
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil pasta and tomato
  2. Blend tomato to make sauce
  3. Fry ground beef on pan
  4. Layer Lasagna with Ingredients
  5. Put on Oven at Hell temperature
  6. Enjoy
